About Us

11+ Years of Excellence in Govt Exam Coaching

Established in 2013, Lakshya Shorthand Typing Institute (LIST) has been the go-to coaching center for thousands of government job aspirants in Faridabad and across Haryana.

We specialize in Hindi and English Shorthand, Typing in multiple languages, and Microsoft Office — all aligned with the latest government exam patterns for SSC, DSSSB, SSB, and Court recruitment.
